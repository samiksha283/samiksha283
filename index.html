About this project

Welcome to Inside-Out, here we give you music recommendations based on your emotion from your facial expression. We use FER model to classify your facial expressions into 7 categories, Happy, Sad, Angry, Disgust, Fear, Surprise and Neutral. Then the SpotifyAPI is used to recommend music based on the detected emotion.
The name of this project comes from the Pixar movie 'Inside-Out' which had separate characters for different emotions, Happy, Sad, Angry, Disgust and Fear.
